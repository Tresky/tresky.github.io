What is the Oviedo Code Camp?

We love Oviedo and the surrounding area and seek to serve the children here by providing high-quality instruction to help your children learn valuable technology skills at a young age.

  • Learn Python

    Your child will be exposed and learn to use a widely used programming language called Python.

  • Create Circuits

    We will be teaching your child how basic electricity works and how to construct simple circuits that are foundational for further learning.

  • Cricital Thinking

    The basis of computer programming is grounded in logic. By learning the basic constructs of programming, your child will learn to think more critically.

  • Engaged Learning

    We believe that learning is most effective when students are hands on with material. To facilitate this, we will be tasking the students with problems to solve on their own.

  • Take Home Electronics

    Each child that attends the Oviedo Code Camp will leave with a beginning's electronics kit to help them continue learning after the summer.

  • Community

    Learning is easier when surrounded by people of similar interests. We will be fostering community between the students attending the camp.
