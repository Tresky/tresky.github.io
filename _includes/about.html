What is the Oviedo Code Camp?

We love Oviedo and the surrounding area and seek to serve the children here by providing high-quality instruction to help your children learn valuable technology skills at a young age.

This summer we are offering two separate classes.

Foundations for Elementary
June 12-16 or June 19-23
Completed 4th or 5th
Students who attend this class will be exposed to foundational knowledge pertaining to how computer programmers think and solve problems.

  • Instructor to student ratio of 1:6 so your child has personal interaction with our staff.
  • Learn how to think like a computer programmer.
  • Receive hands-on learning! Each camper provided with their own computer & equipment.
  • Learn how to program at a basic level in the Python programming language.
  • Apply their Python knowledge to solve robotics challenges using our Edison Robots which provide a fun interface to experiment and learn.
  • Limbitless Solutions, a local non-profit which makes robotic prosthetic arms for kids, will be coming in to work with the campers on the last day.
Foundations for Middle
June 26-30 or July 10-14
Completed 6th through 8th
Students who attend this class will be exposed to foundational knowledge of computer science as well as how circuits function.

  • Instructor to student ratio of 1:6 so your child has personal interaction with our staff.
  • Learn how to think like a computer programmer.
  • Recieve hands-on learning! Each camper provided with their own computer & equipment.
  • Learn how to program at a basic level in the Python programming language.
  • Apply their Python knowledge to solve robotics challenges using our Edison Robots which provide a fun interface to experiment and learn.
  • Students will be instructed in how basic circuitry works and will spend time creating a circuitry project of their own.
  • Limbitless Solutions, a local non-profit which makes robotic prosthetic arms for kids, will be coming in to work with the campers on the last day.
