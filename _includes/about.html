What is the Oviedo Code Camp?

We love Oviedo and the surrounding area and seek to serve the children here by providing high-quality instruction to help your children learn valuable technology skills at a young age. This summer we are offering two separate classes.

Spark
June 12-16 or June 19-23
Completed 4th or 5th

Students who attend this class will be exposed to a lot of foundational knowledge pertaining to computer programs flow and process information. This will be done primarily through the use of the Edison Robots. These provide an easy-to-use interface for learning how programming works at a basic level while allowing the kids to write real code and have it control a real robot.

We will also be bringing in Limbitless Solutions, a local non-profit organization, to talk about the robotic arms that they create and have the students actively engaged in learning how they work as well as programming them.

Ignite
June 26-30 or July 10-14
Completed 6th through 8th

Those who attend this class will be working with the Edison Robots for the first few days of the course. We will be using these to introduce the students to the concepts of programming and how computer scientists write programs to accomplish tasks. This will expose your child to the concept of writing code that controls a robot.

On the last few days of the class, we will be discussing the basics of electronic circuits. We will be instructing the students on how basics components of a circuit fit together to create a product. Your child will receive a beginner's electronics kit and we will be doing some projects with them; they will even get to take the kit home to continue learning after the camp.
